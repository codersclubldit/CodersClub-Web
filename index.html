Coders Club is a coding and technology related club of L.D. College of Engineering. It aims to make the students learn and expertise coding. At Coders Club, the students apply computer programming concepts in solving real life problems.
Who We Are

Who We Are

At Coders Club, the students apply computer programming concepts in solving real life problems. Coders Club focus on new technologies and computer languages and provide technical and practical knowledge to the students.
Mission and Vision

We are thinking for next gen

Driving innovation through code, Pioneering a future powered by technology

Our Mission

The mission is to have integrity and achieve Excellence, respect the Individual and foster an environment of learning and sharing. The focus is on helping students to achieve their career objectives by providing the innovative learning methods and best practical approach of learning.

Our Vision

The vision is to ensure that students acquire and use knowledge, skills to become creative and successful members of society. The club also provides internships to the students to boost up their skills in coding, marketing and management.

Our Achievement

Coders Club triumphs

Embrace our journey of achievement, where innovation and dedication converge. Through the Coders Club's persistent commitment, we've redefined excellence and set new standards in the world of code.

Smart Gujarat for New India hackathon was organised by the state government.

In this hackathon, total 10 teams from the club represented the college and gave an excellent performance. 6 out of these 10 teams reached the final round held at PDPU Gandhinagar. One team emerged out to be the second winner of the competition.

Two out of four teams from the Innovator club won at the Girlscript Hackathon.

One team from the club won at the Dronathon and one at the Enginium Hackathon. Another team also won the Startup Weekend.

Chaxu Valand, Vaibhav Mehta, Vibhav Nirmal, and Adarsh Shah, fourth semester Computer Engineering students, have created an online system for LD College of Engineering. This system was about grievance procedure, which is a machinery to sort out issues between student and college.
